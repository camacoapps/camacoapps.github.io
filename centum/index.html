Centum: The Game

PreviousNext

The goal on Centum is to fill the 100 squares on the board. 

Starting by selecting one and jumping 1 diagonally or 2 horizontally/vertically.

The game ends when you fill the 100 squares or if you run out of moves!

Compete against players all across the world!
Who will get the Centum first?

Earn achievements and challenge
your friends from GameCenter!

Classic 

Free start, 3 wild cards, no time.

Random 

Random start, 3 wild cards,  no time.

Crono 

Free start, no wild cards,  60 seconds.

Master 

Try to reach the goal to grow and earn points.

Available in 5 languages:
English, Spanish, French, German and Italian!

Enjoy the Centum Hit made by Ricardo Martín Castillo.
